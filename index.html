Our story

About Pop Supernova

We believe every child deserves the joy, confidence and friendship that comes from being in a band.

Who we are

Started by two Dads who knew music could do more

Pop Supernova was founded by James and Dan — two dads with primary school-age children who shared a simple but powerful belief: that music teaches children things a traditional classroom often can't.

They saw it with their own children. The shy child who found their voice on stage. The restless child who finally had an outlet. The child who struggled to fit in — until they found their band. Music was building confidence, friendships, resilience and self-belief in ways that went far beyond the notes on a page.

Their mission became clear: make that experience available to every child, in every primary school, regardless of background or ability.

The founding team

Meet the people behind Pop Supernova

A team built from real passion — for music, for children and for making a difference.

James

Co-Founder & Founding Band Leader

A musician for over 30 years, James has dedicated his life to the power of music. After a fulfilling career as a traditional music teacher — where he witnessed every day how music could unlock something in a child — he moved into business before deciding to combine both worlds. As a dad watching his own children grow up, he knew there was a better, bolder way to bring music into schools. Pop Supernova is the result of that conviction.

What drives us

Our mission

We believe that music is one of the most powerful tools for child development — but for too many children, it remains out of reach. Traditional classroom settings don't work for every child. Some children need to do, to play, to create.

Pop Supernova exists to change that. By bringing small, structured, joyful band sessions into primary schools, we give every child — regardless of background, ability or experience — the chance to discover what music can do for them.

It isn't just about learning an instrument. It's about the child who stands a little taller after their first performance. The one who makes their first real friendship in a band. The one who finally finds something they're proud of.

What to expect

Your child's Pop Supernova journey

Each week, your child will attend a 30-minute band session at school with up to 8 other children. They'll choose an instrument, pick a band name and learn popular songs — all while building skills, friendships and confidence.

  • 30-minute weekly session during the school day
  • Small bands of up to 8 children
  • No previous experience needed
  • Drums, guitar, keyboard or vocals
  • End-of-term performances each year
  • Track your child's progress via our parent portal
